Citizenship

Being a productive member of society and fulfilling the responsiblilies that come with that.

[2]

Honesty

  • Not deceiving in words or actions
  • Honesty allows society to function with minimal misunderstandings.

Compassion

  • Sympathy and wanting to help others.
  • Compassion means everyone in the society helps each other when things are bad

Respect

  • Acknowledging other's worth, influence, and strengths.
  • Respect allows people to interact as equals in a society.

Responsibility

  • Being accountable for something within one's power, control, or management.[1]
  • Responsibility ensures people are reliable in a community.

Courage

  • Doing what you believe in despite opposition.
  • Courage lets people stand up to one another in a society if they disagree.

Selflessness

  • Putting the needs of others above your own.
  • Selflessness encourages the community to work together and ook out for one another.
